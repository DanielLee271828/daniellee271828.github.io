**Problem 1.** ([2024 F=ma Q15](https://www.aapt.org/physicsteam/2024/upload/2024_F-ma_Solutions.pdf)) The viscous force between two plates of area $A$, with relative speed $v$ and separation $d$, is $F=\eta Av/d$, where $\eta$ is the viscosity. In fluid mechanics, the Ohnesorge number is a dimensionless number proportional to $\eta$ which characterizes the importance of viscous forces, in a drop of fluid of density $\rho$, surface tension $\gamma$, and length scale $l$. Which of the following could be the definition of the Ohnesorge number?

(A) $\frac{\eta l}{\rho \gamma}$			(B) $\eta l\frac{\rho}{\gamma}$		(C) $\eta\frac{\rho}{\gamma l}$
(D) $\eta \frac{\rho l}{\gamma}$		(E) $\frac{\eta}{\sqrt{\rho \gamma l}}$

(Hint: SI unit of surface tension is $Nm^{-1}$ and SI unit of viscosity is $kg m/s^2$)
